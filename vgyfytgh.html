There are two types of Unemployment:-


1.Disguised Unemployment:

Disguised Unemployment is a kind of unemployment in which there are people who are visibly employed but are actually unemployed. This situation is also known as Hidden Unemployment. In such a situation more people are engaged in a work than required.

Foe Example=If a piece of land requires only three people to work on it and instead five people are working on it, then the two extra people are said to be in a situation of disguised unemployment.



2.Seasonal Unemployment:

Seasonal unemployment occurs when people are unemployed at particular times of the year when demand for labour is lower than usual. Since only a few workers are needed, it results in unemployment.

For example= in agriculture, the sowing and harvesting periods require labour, but during the rest of the year, the landless labour may remain out of work.
